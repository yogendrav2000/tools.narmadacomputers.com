Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

1. 100% Client-Side Privacy Sandbox

At Narmada Tools, we take your data privacy with absolute seriousness. Unlike traditional utility websites that upload files, codes, or text blocks to remote backend servers for processing, Narmada Tools operates 100% locally in your web browser.

All operations—including PDF merging, image compression, code formatting, hashing, mathematical calculations, and parsing—use client-side JavaScript APIs (such as the File Reader and HTML5 Canvas). Your documents, input text, and image assets are never transmitted across the network, stored on our servers, or shared with third parties.

2. Use of Local Browser Storage

We utilize standard HTML5 Local Storage (localStorage) on your computer solely to improve your user experience:

  • Theme Preferences: Storing whether you prefer dark mode or light mode to prevent theme flickering when navigating between utility pages.
  • Offline Subscriptions: Logging local newsletter subscriptions offline before syncing when internet connection permits.
  • Local Telemetry: Storing local usage statistics to dynamically show popular or featured utility lists on the homepage.

3. Analytics & Telemetry

To analyze site usage and maintain performance, we track basic anonymous telemetry. This includes information such as pages visited, timestamps, referrer URLs, and browser types. None of this telemetry contains personally identifiable information, file contents, or input fields processed by the tools.

4. Cookies and Advertising

We may partner with third-party networks, such as Google AdSense, to display non-intrusive advertisements. These networks may use cookies (like the DART cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to our site and other sites on the internet. You can manage or disable cookie settings in your individual browser preferences.

5. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the security of our browser-based utility hub, feel free to contact us via the contact details provided on our main domain.
