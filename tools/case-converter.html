What is Sentence Case?

Sentence Case capitalizes only the first letter of each sentence, keeping the rest of the text in lowercase. Perfect for copy pasting raw notes into email formats.

What is Title Case?

Title Case capitalizes all primary words in a title, leaving minor words (articles like a, an, the, and coordinating conjunctions like but, and, or) in lowercase. Ideal for blog headings.
